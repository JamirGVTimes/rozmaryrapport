BACKGROUND

Rozmary Rapport Foundation (RRF) was founded in 2019 by a servant of God Birungi Charles from Africa, Uganda - Hoima district. It started by working on Children Empowerment with a purpose of improving the livelihoods of the caregivers and thier children through food security, with HIV/AIDS prevention and education.
He witnessed the suffering of so many abandoned children and orphans and felt that something had to be done to help them. Out of his unswerving conviction, Rozmary Rapport Foundation idea was born.
